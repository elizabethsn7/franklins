The Franklin Room

Comfortable. Convivial.

The Franklin Room can be used for a wide variety of events from personal to corporate and can be arranged to suit your needs.

We want your event to be exactly what you want. Order off the menu or we can customize our menu for your event.

Planning an event or meeting?

Check out our High Definition Projector with a 10' wide drop down digital projector screen along with easy to use plug in connectivity. Our room has a separate HVAC system so you can control the temperature.

Need a little more privacy? You decide, our custom glass barn doors can remain open or closed for whatever you need.

Please know we have a "minimum spend" amount if booking the room on Fridays and Saturdays.
